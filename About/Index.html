What this project is

Dimensional Scripture is a research-driven examination of consciousness, dimensional reality, and the biblical text. It begins from a simple premise: ancient authors were describing real experiences and real phenomena using the language available to them.

The thesis

This project argues that strict materialism cannot account for consciousness or the full range of human experience. It proposes that the biblical text reflects a consistent worldview of layered reality, and that modern research increasingly echoes assumptions the text already contains.

This does not attempt to prove theology with science. It examines where their descriptions converge.
